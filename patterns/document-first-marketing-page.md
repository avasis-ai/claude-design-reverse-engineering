# Pattern: document-first marketing page

## Observation

High-signal product pages often open with a **two-sentence hook**, followed by **three parallel takeaways** (insight / evidence / action), then deeper material. Comparison tables appear before long prose dumps.

## When to use

- Single-page sites for developer tools.
- Release posts where readers skim on phones first.

## Anti-patterns

- Hero paragraphs longer than five sentences without a summary card.
- Feature grids before the reader knows *why* the product exists.
