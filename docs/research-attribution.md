# Research notes and attribution

This project synthesizes **publicly discussable** heuristics for how strong assistant answers tend to be structured: hierarchy, chunking, progressive disclosure, tables, and signposting.

Primary synthesis for the first release was informed by a private working document:

- `claudeDesignReverseEngineering.md` (author’s local notes; not vendored here by default)

If you maintain a fork, you may add sanitized excerpts under `docs/private/` **only** when you have rights to redistribute them.

Community contributions should cite:

- **What** was observed (surface: chat transcript, marketing page, docs),
- **When** (approximate date),
- **Why** it matters (mapping to a pattern card or checklist item).

This repository does **not** claim to reproduce Anthropic system prompts, proprietary specifications, or non-public product behavior.
