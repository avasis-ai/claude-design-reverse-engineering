# Contributing

This repository documents **observable, user-visible patterns** in long-form assistant outputs and related UX heuristics. It is **not** a place for proprietary leaks.

- Prefer evidence you have the right to share (your own screenshots, your own outputs, public marketing surfaces).
- Label uncertainty. Use `proposed-` prefixes for speculative token or pattern names.
- Keep pull requests small and reviewable. New pattern notes go under `patterns/` with a short “observation basis” section.
- By contributing, you agree your contributions are licensed under the same terms as this repository (MIT).

See the ethics paragraph in `README.md` for boundaries.
